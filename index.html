About

Neuro​Dudes is a limited collection that originated in the digital universe through close collaboration between artificial and human intelligence. Each painting is a blend of the styles of outstanding artists. Each Dude is unique, as is the landscape he is in. This collection was created using neural networks with VQGAN + CLIP architecture and with an immense passion for generative art.

We sell 100 arts on OpenSea at 0.015ETH apiece.

FAQ

What is the Neuro​Dudes collection?

The Neuro​Dudes are a limited collection of AI paintings. The collection consists of 100 unique NFT tokens. Each picture was created using neural networks with VQGAN + CLIP architecture and is absolutely unique.

How can I purchase these tokens?

You can buy them on OpenSea, or follow us on Twitter and Discord and participate in Giveaways.

What is the price of each NFT?

Each NFT costs 0.015ETH on OpenSea. If you want to buy several items, you can contact us on Twitter or Discord and negotiate a discount.

What are the prospects of the authors?

We are planning to create new experimental projects related to AI and NFT. For example, we are going to combine AI and real physics experiments. Also, we want to build a community of people who are interested in it.
